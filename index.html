About Us

Dial n Dine is a national call center geared to meet telephonic ordering and customers' needs. The main focus of the call center is to keep existing clients & gain new ones by giving them ultimate service satisfaction. However, the call center does not focus only on customers but also relieves the pressure from different stores that offer food delivery/collection services. This particular call center takes calls for Debonairs, Steers, Wimpy, and Fishaways. These are all franchises of Famous Brands
